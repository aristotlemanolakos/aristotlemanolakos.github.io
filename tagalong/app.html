The get together & get to know you app.

Tagalong is an easily navigatable application used to either find groups or host a group doing an activity! This application lets you reach a vast audience and curate a face to face social event using only your smartphone.



  • TagAlong can enable you to meet new friends or collaboate with an existing friend group.


  • After a few texts tagalong lets you quickly coordinate a meeting with people in your area or lets you join a premade activity.


  • Tagalong allows you to save money by going with a group and mitigating costs!
